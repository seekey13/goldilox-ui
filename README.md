# Goldilox-UI

[Goldilox](https://github.com/dewiniaid/ffxi-ashita-goldilox) is an Ashita4 addon exclusively for the [CatsEye XI](https://www.catseyexi.com/) custom daily content 
consisting of goblin dailies, including the goblin by the same name.  Originally developed by [dewiniaid](https://github.com/dewiniaid/ffxi-ashita-goldilox/commits?author=dewiniaid).


Goldilox-UI is my spin on it to consolidate the information into an imgui panel.  To make the process more visual.  

Each quest gets it's own progress bar to track kills or items.   If all objectives have been met the bar will turn gray, and vanish when completed.

`/huh motion` link will issue the command then parse the positional text response and display `Far East` in the panel for example.

`#sea` is a simple hyperlink to the Sea channel on the CatsEyeXI Discord where Flux locations are discussed.

All the other `Green Text Links` will navigate you to the [FFXI Wiki](https://www.bg-wiki.com/ffxi/) page for additional information.  Just a quick way to figure things out, if you are not familar with the Mob/Zone/Item.

Sea Organs and Beetrix items are are detected/tracked in your inventory 

## Usage

Load Goldilox-UI with `/addon load goldilox-ui`.  With the addon loaded, talk to Goblins and Palalumin to track what your dailies are.  If you are grouping up be sure to be in Party before talking to NPCs to ensure the quests align.

Leave the addon loaded while working on said dailies (i.e. killing required creatures or finding the secret chest).

Daily status resets at JP Midnight.
